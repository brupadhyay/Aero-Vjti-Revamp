    About Us
    We are the aero-modelling club of Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute, Mumbai. Our aim is to spread knowledge and enkindle interest in RC aircraft designing and modelling in the young and curious minds of society.
    Achievements
    In order to test our skills and knowledge, we participate in various national and international competitions, achieving top ranks.
    Workshops
    To spread knowledge of aero-modelling, we conduct various workshops for school and college students. These workshops comprise of lectures, discussions, tutorials of various software and develop construction skills.
    Technical Advancements
    We use and develop several algorithms and methods for aerodynamic and structural analyses of components for aircraft-building.
